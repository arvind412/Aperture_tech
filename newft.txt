<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="India-related_terms">India-related terms</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bharat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: India-related terms">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bharata_Khanda" title="Bharata Khanda">Bharata Khanda</a>, the original Sanskrit name for the Indian subcontinent</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bh%C4%81rat_Ga%E1%B9%87ar%C4%81jya" class="mw-redirect" title="Bhārat Gaṇarājya">Bhārat Gaṇarājya</a> or simply "Bharat", for the <i>Republic of India</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bharata_Mata" class="mw-redirect" title="Bharata Mata">Bharata Mata</a> (Mother India), the national personification of India as a mother</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bharat_Ratna" title="Bharat Ratna">Bharat Ratna</a>, India's highest civilian award</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bharata_Natyam" class="mw-redirect" title="Bharata Natyam">Bharata Natyam</a>, a classical dance form.</li>
</ul>
